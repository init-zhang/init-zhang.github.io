- [Bear](https://bearblog.dev/): privacy-first, no-nonsense, super-fast blogging platform with an updating discover page
- [Midnight Pub](https://midnight.pub/): virtual pub that lets users write posts and create pages
- [Blogroll Club](https://blogroll.club/): diverse and comprehensive collection of blogs and personal sites
- [Bukmark Club](https://bukmark.club/): collection of personal websites with a curated collection of links to other websites
